Rate Limit Rules (subject to change with or without notice):

  • Installations/downloads of Chocolatey itself are rate limited at about 5 per minute per IP address - temporary ban expires after 1 hour.
  • All other packages are rate limited at about 20 per minute per IP address - temporary ban expires after 1 hour.

How Do I Avoid Being Rate Limited?

  • Individual? please reach out and give us a heads up - we are constantly adjusting limits to get to a happy medium. In addition to providing the requested information, be sure to also mention "individual use" and provide details on what you were doing that caused the rate limiting. We may ask you to provide logs for further inspection.
  • Organizational Use? Implement Chocolatey organizational deployment best practices. Installation of Chocolatey itself and everything else should be from your internal repository and not directly from the community package repository. With 15-30 minutes you can implement a "proxy" repository which automatically caches (but does not internalize) packages from the community repository (https://chocolatey.org/api/v2). This provides the same experience you get in using the community repository now but with more availability and no rate limiting!
